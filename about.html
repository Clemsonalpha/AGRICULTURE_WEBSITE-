About NecsTrade

Building bridges between Ghana's agricultural producers and global markets since our inception.

Our Story

From local beginnings to international reach

Building Agricultural Bridges

Founded in Accra, Ghana, NecsTrade began with a simple mission: to connect the rich agricultural resources of Ghana with global markets that demand quality and reliability.

What started as a local commodity trading operation has grown into a comprehensive agricultural trading company serving clients across West Africa and beyond.

Today, we handle thousands of tonnes of agricultural products annually, maintaining strong relationships with farmers, processors, and international buyers.

Our Mission

To be the most reliable and trusted agricultural commodity trading partner in West Africa, creating sustainable value for farmers, clients, and communities through transparent and efficient trading practices.

We aim to enhance food security and economic development by connecting quality agricultural producers with markets that appreciate and reward their efforts.

Our Vision

To transform Ghana into a premier hub for agricultural commodity trading in Africa, recognized globally for quality, reliability, and innovation in agricultural supply chains.

We envision a future where Ghanaian agricultural products are the first choice for quality-conscious buyers worldwide, driving prosperity for all stakeholders in the value chain.

End-to-End Trading Solutions

  • Quality Assurance: Rigorous inspection and grading at every stage

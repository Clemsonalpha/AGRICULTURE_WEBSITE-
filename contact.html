Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our agricultural commodity trading services

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum quantities vary by product. For bulk commodities like maize and soyabeans, we typically handle orders from 10 tonnes upwards. For poultry and livestock products, we can accommodate smaller quantities based on availability.

Do you export internationally?

Yes, we export agricultural commodities to various international markets. We handle all necessary documentation, quality certifications, and logistics for international shipments.

What quality standards do you maintain?

We maintain rigorous quality control standards. All products are inspected and graded according to international standards. We provide quality certificates and can arrange for third-party inspections if required.

How do you handle logistics and delivery?

We coordinate complete logistics including transportation, storage, and delivery. For international shipments, we work with reliable shipping partners to ensure timely and secure delivery.

Can you supply on a regular contract basis?

Yes, we offer regular supply contracts for businesses that require consistent commodity supply. Contact us to discuss contract terms and arrangements.

What payment terms do you offer?

We offer flexible payment terms including letters of credit, bank transfers, and other secure payment methods. Terms are negotiated based on the order size and customer relationship.
